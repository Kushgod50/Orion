How to use Orion
Your AI-powered creator studio — ideas, SEO, content planning, and thumbnail feedback all in one place.
1
Enter your niche
Fill in the setup panel below. Be specific — "home workouts for busy moms" gets far better results than just "fitness". Add your platform and goals too.
2
Pick a tab & generate
Choose Video Ideas, SEO & Tags, Content Planner, or Thumbnail AI. Hit the gold Generate button and Orion calls the AI instantly.
3
Copy & publish
Click any title, description, or tag to copy it instantly. Paste directly into YouTube Studio, TikTok, or wherever you publish. Download your plan as a PDF.
✦
Video Ideas
Generate ideas, click a card to select it, and a popup appears with a shortcut to the SEO tab. Click again to deselect.
↑
SEO & Tags
Get optimised titles, a 150-200 word description, and 15 tags. Click any item to copy it to clipboard instantly.
☰
Content Planner
Add your platforms, follower counts, and growth goal. Orion sets your ideal posting frequency and builds a full roadmap with video ideas per period. Download as PDF.
◆
Thumbnail AI — Premium
Upload your thumbnail. Get scored on contrast, readability, emotional impact, and mobile clarity. Every weakness gets a specific fix.
Pro tips
✦Ideas → SEO shortcut. Click an idea card, then hit "Open in SEO". The title auto-fills so you generate the full package in one click.
✦Titles under 60 chars. YouTube cuts off longer titles in search. The character count next to each title helps you pick the right one.
✦Real follower counts matter. Even if small. The planner calibrates to where you are now, not where you want to be.
✦Click periods to expand. In the Content Planner, each week/month/quarter is clickable and shows the video ideas for that period.
