Quick start — 3 steps to your first result
1
Enter your niche
Type what your channel is about in the setup panel below. Specific beats vague — "budget travel Southeast Asia" gets far better results than just "travel".
2
Pick your tab
Use Video Ideas to brainstorm, SEO & Tags to optimise, Content Planner to build a growth roadmap, or Thumbnail AI to improve your click-through rate.
3
Generate & copy
Hit the gold Generate button. Results appear below. Click any title, description, or tag to copy it to your clipboard instantly — ready to paste into YouTube Studio.
How each tab works
✦
Video Ideas
Choose how many ideas to generate (free = 1, upgrade for more). Each result shows a title, hook sentence, content type, difficulty, and viral potential rating. Click any card to select it — a gold popup appears with a direct link to the SEO tab. Click the card again to deselect it.
↑
SEO & Tags
Type your video topic manually, or select an idea from the Ideas tab to auto-fill it. Pick how many title options you want. You'll get YouTube-optimised titles with character counts, a full keyword-rich description ready to paste, and 15 search tags. Click any item to copy it.
☰
Content Planner
Select Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly. Check every platform you're active on and enter your follower count for each. Set a specific growth goal (e.g. "reach 5k YouTube subs in 60 days") and posting frequency. Orion builds you a personalised roadmap with days, hooks, content types, and growth strategies.
◆
Thumbnail AI — Premium
Upload your thumbnail image (PNG or JPG, 1280x720 ideal). Orion scores it out of 100 across four dimensions: text readability, visual contrast, emotional impact, and mobile clarity. Every weakness comes with a specific, actionable fix so you know exactly what to change before you publish.
Tips for better results
✦ Be as specific as possible. "Home workouts for busy moms over 40 who hate the gym" will generate far more targeted ideas than just "fitness". The niche field is the most important input on the page.
✦ Use the Ideas → SEO shortcut. After generating ideas, click a card and hit "Open in SEO & Tags". The title auto-fills and you can generate the full SEO package in one click — no typing needed.
✦ For the Content Planner, enter your real follower counts even if they're small. Orion calibrates the strategy to where you actually are — a plan for 200 followers looks very different from one for 20k.
✦ Aim for titles under 60 characters. The character count shown next to each generated title helps you pick the right one. YouTube shows roughly 60 chars in search results before cutting off.
